Student Rest CRUD Operations:

Get All Students: localhost:8081/students
Get Student Based on RollNo: localhost:8081/student/1
Create Student: localhost:8081/student/add
Update the Student Details: localhost:8081/student/update/2
Delete Student based on ID: localhost:8081/student/delete/1
